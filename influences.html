How Are People Being Influenced in Voting Decisions?

Every election, millions of people cast their votes, influenced by a variety of factors—some obvious, others hidden beneath the surface. Voting decisions are rarely made in isolation; they are shaped by a combination of personal values, external forces, and cultural contexts. This page introduces you to the major factors influencing voter behavior, helping you explore why people vote the way they do.
